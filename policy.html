Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 27, 2024

Overview

Welcome to Kerkuk Kitabevi. We value your privacy and are committed to protecting any information you share with us. This policy explains our straightforward approach to information handling and privacy protection.

Our No-Collection Commitment

We believe in minimal data collection. Our website (kerkukkitabevi.net) does not collect, store, or process any visitor information. This means we do not collect:

  • IP addresses (IPv4 or IPv6)
  • Browser information
  • Device information
  • Cookies
  • Usage data
  • Location data
  • Any tracking or analytics information

Essential Information Collection

We only collect information in two specific situations:

1. When You Make a Purchase

To fulfill your order, we collect only:

  • Your name
  • Email address and/or phone number
  • Shipping address (for physical deliveries)
  • Purchase details

2. Club Membership

If you join our book club, we collect:

  • Your name
  • Email address
  • Any additional information you voluntarily provide for membership purposes

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information solely for:

  • Processing and fulfilling your orders
  • Delivering your purchases
  • Communicating about your order or membership
  • Sending important updates about your purchase or membership

Data Protection

  • We store your information securely
  • We keep purchase and membership information only as long as necessary
  • We never sell or share your information with third parties
  • We use industry-standard security measures to protect your data

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request information about your data we hold
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Update or correct your information
  • Opt out of communications
